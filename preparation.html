Preparing Matcha

Everything you need to make the perfect bowl of Matcha

SPOON


Measure 1 to 2 tablespoons Matcha and a few drops of water to form a paste.

Learn more >

WHISK


Add 8 ounces of water just off the boil, whisking vigorously in a zig zag motion until the lumps are gone and the tea is frothy.

Learn more >

BOWL


A bowl tends to be easier to mix in than a cup. If clumping occurs consider sifting the tea before adding water. To serve drink directly from the bowl, or transfer to a cup.

Learn more >

Our 80 bristle bamboo whisks have the longest life expectancy when washed thoroughly after each use and positioned on the included bamboo holder to dry
